WHY STARTED A UJJWALA YOJANA

             Even today there are many houses in our country which do not have cooking gas. Due to which they have to face a lot of difficulties. Keeping this in mind, the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana has been launched by the Central Government on 1 May 2016.Through Pradhan Mantri ujjwala Yojana, LPG will be provided to the APL, BPL and ration card holder women of the country. The scheme will be operated by the Union Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas. Through this article, you will be able to get information related to the process of applying under PM Ujjwala Yojana. Apart from this, you will also be made aware of the eligibility and purpose of Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Scheme. You will not only be able to get complete information about PMUY Scheme by reading this article of ours.

REQUIRED DOCUMENT FOR UJJWALA YOJANA

Required documents for fill the application form under the scheme Their list is given in the following way....

  • BPL certificate issued by Municipality President (Urban Area) / Panchayat Pradhan (Rural Area).
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar card or Voter ID card).
  • BPL Ration Card 4.Aadhaar number of all family members.
  • Passport size photograph.
  • Address proof.
  • caste certificate.
  • Jan Dhan Bank Account Details / Bank Passbook.
  • 14 point declaration in the prescribed format to be signed by the applicant.

OBJECTIVES OF UJJWALA YOJANA

The main objective of the scheme is to provide deposit free LPG connections to women from poor households. The Government had set a target of 5 Crore LPG connections to be distributed to the poor households under the scheme.

  1. Improved health of women and children.
  2. Additional employment generation.
