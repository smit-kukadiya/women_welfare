WHY STARTED A MAHILA SHAKTI KENDARA YOJANA

             Skill development scheme for self-reliance of minorities, scheduled castes / tribes, backward castes and women of the country by the government, scheme to provide loans at subsidized rates for self-employment and a scheme to increase awareness for nutrition and rights of women and children. has gone. In the same sequence, in the budget of 2017-18, it was announced to include “Pradhan Mantri Mahila Shakti Kendra Yojana or Nari Shakti Scheme” under the women’s safety and empowerment mission.
             Pradhan Mantri Mahila Shakti Kendra Scheme Details – On 22nd November 2017, the cabinet ministry led by PM-Modi has approved “Mahila Shakti Kendra Yojana”. Under this scheme, women from rural areas of backward districts will be focused.

             Will be done. Along with this scheme, the cabinet also said that Beti Bachao Beti Padhao scheme, one stop center scheme for sexually assaulted women and 7 more schemes will be extended in future. Under this scheme rural women will be empowered through community participation so that they can realize their full potential.

OBJECTIVES OF MAHILA SHAKTI KENDRA YOJANA

The scheme is framed for the empowerment of women who belong to the rural community. These women will be provided with the essential impetus to establish smaller business units. Moreover, the scheme is intended to create awareness of the roles to be played by these women and about the schemes and programs launched by the government.

  1. PMMSK Scheme will empower rural women through community participation to create an environment in which they realize their full potential.
  2. PMMSKs will be set up across 115 most backward districts of the country in order to reach out to rural women and facilitate health, nutrition, skill development, employment, digital literacy, etc.
